PSVM — Predictive SVM Ensemble Strategy

A walk-forward algorithmic trading system for the S&P 500 using an ensemble of Support Vector Regression models trained on 30+ technical indicators spanning trend, momentum, volatility, and volume. Individual model signals are aggregated via majority vote, with per-window threshold optimization via Bayesian search (Optuna). Backtested 1998–2024: 14.86% CAGR, 1.58 Sharpe ratio, -18.25% max drawdown — with notably strong crisis-period performance in 2001, 2008, and 2022.
